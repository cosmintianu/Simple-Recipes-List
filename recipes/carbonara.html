Pasta carbonara

Ingredients

  • 220g Spaghetti or Spaghettoni
  • 25g Guanciale or pancetta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pan of boiling salted water until al dente.Meanwhile, heat the oil in a pan and fry the guanciale or pancetta until crisp.
  2. Lightly beat the eggs in a large bowl with the grated cheese and pepper. When the pasta is ready, drain and add to the pan with the guanciale.
  3. Then mix well to coat everything. Take off the heat. Allow to cool slightly. Then add the egg and cheese mixture. Stir to coat the pasta and serve immediately.
